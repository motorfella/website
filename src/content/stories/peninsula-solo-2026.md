I left KL on a Sunday morning with one plan: ride the whole thing.

No fixed stops. No GPS route locked in. Just the map, the tank, and five days of freedom.

Day one was easy — north on the PLUS, watching the city disappear in my mirrors. Day three was where it got real. The east coast roads are another world. Quieter. Slower. More honest.

By day five I rolled back into KL sunburned, tired, and completely satisfied.

This is why we ride.
